Tests - This is used so you can make sure everything is working good before you take on the caves.

1. Click on "Use UH on self" to make the tool try to use a UH Rune on you, if it fails, go to step 3.

2. Click on "Use SD on target" to make the tool try to use an SD Rune on your target, if it fails, go to step 3.

3. If the messages under the buttons are red, open your rune backpacks and click on "Find Runes" and the bot will locate them, the ones that are green are ready to use.

 

Hotkeys - This tool will use keys that you don't need for Tibia so you can shoot runes at your target by simply pressing a button.

1. Click on "Set Hotkeys" to choose which hotkeys you want to activate. If there is a mark next to one of them, it is already on.

2. Click on "Activate AIM Bot hotkeys in this client." to turn the Hotkeys that you selected on for the client you are using.
