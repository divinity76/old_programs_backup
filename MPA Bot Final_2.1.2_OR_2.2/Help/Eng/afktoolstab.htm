Spell Caster - This tool will make you cast spells while you are away from your computer so your magic level will go up.

1. In the "Spell" box, type the words that you need to say for your spell (i.e. Utevo Lux)

2. In the "Mana Needed" box, type the amount of mana the spell you want to cast will use.

3. Click on "Whisper Spell" and your character will whisper whenever you say the spell.

4. Click on "Active" to turn on the Spell Caster.

 

Auto Eater - This will make your character automatically eat the food that you have.

Backpack with food must be open!

1. The number in the "Delay" box is the number of milliseconds before it will eat another piece of food. (i.e. 8000 milliseconds will eat 1 piece of food every 8 seconds).

2. By clicking on "Eat Fishes Under" you will eat any fish that is on the ground underneath your character.

3. Click on "Active" to turn on the Auto Eater.

 

Random Fishing - This will make your character randomly fish in water that you are near.

Backpack with Fishing Rod must be open!

Rune Maker - This tool will make your character create runes while you are away from your computer.

Backpacks with Blank Runes must be open!

1. In the "Spell" box, type in the words that you need to say for your spell (i.e. Adori Gran)

2. In the "Mana" box, type the amount of mana that you need to make the rune.

3. Click on "Left Hand" if you want your character to make runes in the hand thats furthest left on your screen, or on "Right Hand" if you want the runes made with the hand furthest right on your screen.

Make sure the hand is empty!

5. Select "Whisper" if you want your character to whisper the words for the spell when he casts it.

6. Click on "Active" to turn on the Rune Maker.

 

Auto Healer - This tool will heal your character when he reaches a set amount of HP.

Backpack with UH runes must be open!

1. In the "Use UH or spell when hp is below" box, type the amount of health you must reach to be healed. (i.e. 200 will heal you when your HP drops down to 200).

2. If you are going to use both a spell and runes, click on the circle next to the one that the bot should try to use first.

3. Click on the square next to "UH Rune" if you want to be healed with a UH, click on the square next to "Spell" if you want to be healed with a spell.

4. In the "Spell" box, enter the words of the healing spell you want to use if you are going to use one (i.e. Exura Vita)

5. Click on "Active" to turn on the Auto Healer.
