Rainbow Outfit - This tool will cause your character to randomly change colors very quickly.

Please note that by using this tool your are almost guaranteed to get banned!

Premium vocations are not working for free accounts!

1. Under the "Outfit" drop-down menu, select what your character type is.

2. Click on "Active" to turn on the Rainbow Outfit tool.

 

Hided Monsters - This tool will make it so you can see monsters that turn invisible (i.e. Stalkers)

1. By clicking on the "Show Invisible monsters" button, you will be able to see invisible creatures.

2. Click on "Active above functions" to turn the "Hided Monsters" tool on.

 

Trade Helper - This tool is used to send messages to the trade channel without having to worry about re-sending every 2 minutes.

1. In the "Text to say every 2 minutes on trade channel" box, type in the message you want to say in the trade channel.

2. Click on "Active" to turn on the Trade Helper.

 

Spear Grabber - This tool is used by Paladins to pick up their spears that they have thrown next to them while training.

1. On the grid of squares, your character is the middle square, select the square that is where the spears will land.

2. By clicking on "Select All" all of the squares will be selected for you and the "Select All" will change to "Deselect All"

3. By clicking on "Deselect All" all of the squares will be un-marked for you and the "Deselect All" will change to "Select All"

4. Click on either "Left Hand" or "Right Hand" to choose which spot to pick up spears to.

5. Change the "Delay" box to set how often the bot will try to pick up spears. (1000 = every 1 second)

6. Under the "Pick Up" drop-down menu, you can select what type of weapon you are using.

7. Click on "Active" to turn on the Spear Grabber.
