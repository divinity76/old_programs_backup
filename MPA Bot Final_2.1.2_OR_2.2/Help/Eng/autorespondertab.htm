Auto Responder - Auto Reponder is very useful tool that will automaticly answer questions from other people.

1. In Question text box write question (i.e 'why*').

2. In Answer text box write answer for the question in the question text box (i.e 'i dont know').

3. Click "Add Respond". After it your question and answer has been added to the list. Now make more of them (about 10 or more if you want). When you finish go to next step.

4. Now when you have about 10 or more things then click Active. Now when someone on your screen say your question then your character will automaticly say the answer after: (Length(Answer) * 300ms) + Random(2000ms). 1000 ms = 1 second.

Remember! You can use * character for example look at below examples:

why*, *noob, *hunted*, *?

As you can see. You can make a lot of them without any limit. The good thing is you can load those lists or even save them.
