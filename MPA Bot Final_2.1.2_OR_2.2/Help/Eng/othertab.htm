Movement - This tool can be used to make your character walk around in a general area.

1. The buttons that are in a square shape will make you move in the direction that you click on.

2. By selecting "Random Move" your character will walk around in random directions until you de-select it.

 

Turn - This tool lets you make your character rotate in place, can be used to "Dance" as some call it.

1. By selecting one of the 4 directional buttons on the tool, you will turn and face that direction.

2. By selecting "Random Turn" your character will automatically start spinning in place and won't stop until you turn off this button.

 

Reload Tool - This tool will reload your ammunition when you run out of distance weapons.

Backpack with ammo to reload must be open!

1. By using the "Reload Item" drop-down menu, you can select the type of ammo to reload.

2. Click on "Active" to turn on the Reload Tool.

 

Experience Calculator - This will keep track of the amount of Experience you gain while playing your current turn of Tibia.

1. "Start Experience" shows the amount of total experience you had when you first logged in.

2. "Current Experience" shows the amount that you have at the present time.

3. "Experience Done" shows the total amount that you have gained in your current Tibia session.

4. By clicking on "Refresh" you will make the calculator check itself to make sure it is accurate.

5. By clicking on "Reset" you will cause the calculator to think that you have just logged on and it will change to the proper numbers.

 

Other - These are other tools that didn't fall into any category.

1. By clicking the "Anti-Kick" box, you will make it so the bot won't let your character log out from being idle for 15 minutes.

2. The "Players on Screen" will tell you how many people are on your screen at the current time.

3. By clicking on "Check All Floors", the bot will check the players on your screen and the players above and below you.

4. By clicking on "Refresh" the bot will count the players on screen for you again and re-set the number in the box if it needs to be changed.
