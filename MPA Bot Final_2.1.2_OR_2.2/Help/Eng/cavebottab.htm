Waypoints list - This list will show you the current places that your Cavebot will walk.

 

Right Half - This area has all of the tools you need to make your Cavebot run.

1. By clicking on "Insert Position" the spot that your character is currently standing on will be added to the list of spots to walk to.

2. By clicking on "Delete Selected" the spot that you have clicked on in the Waypoints List will be taken out of the list.

3. By clicking on "Move Up" the spot that you have clicked on in the Waypoints List will be moved up 1 place in the list.

4. By clicking on "Move Down" the spot that you have clicked on in the Waypoints List will be moved down 1 place in the list.

5. By clicking on "Delete All" the whole Waypoints List will be erased so you can start fresh.

6. The "Step: #" box will tell you which step you are on in the Cavebot.

7. By clicking on "Save Waypoints" your bot will save the current Waypoints List so you can re-use it next time you need to.

8. By clicking on "Load Waypoints" your bot will load the Waypoints List from a previous time and you can use it again.

9. By clicking on "Use Item" the character will use item on spot that your character is currently standing. Before you click "Use Item" make sure you have clicked "Insert Position" button while standing on the spot (i.e Ladder).

10. By clicking on "Use Rope" the character will use rope on spot that your character is currently standing. Before you click "Use Item" make sure you have clicked "Insert Position" button while standing on rope spot.

 

Tools - These are the more specific Cavebot commands.

1. By selecting "Attack Monsters" your character will attack all monsters that he comes by while walking through the cave.

2. By selecting "Walk Between Waypoints" your character will walk through the different spots in your Waypoints List.

3. By selecting "Loop waypoint" your character will continue to loop through all of the places in the Waypoints List until you turn off the Cavebot.

4. By selecting "Loot Gold Coins" your character will collect gold coins to your backpack from dead monsters.

5. By selecting "Eat food in body" your character will eat all types of food inside dead monsters.

6. Click on "Active" to turn on the Cavebot.

 

Record Way - These features are useful while adding waypoints.

You cannot record multi-floor ways!

1. By clicking on "Record Way" the waypoints will be automaticly added while walking.

2. By clicking on "Stop Record" the recording will be stopped.
