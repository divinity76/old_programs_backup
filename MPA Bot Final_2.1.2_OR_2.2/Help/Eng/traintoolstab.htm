Train Tools - This is used to make your character attack a creature without killing it, so you can train indefinitely.

1. In the "Partner Name" box, type the name of the person/creature that you are attacking. (i.e. Monk)

2. In the "Start attack partner when his HP (%) is bigger than" box, enter the amount of HP in percent your partner should have when you want your character to start attacking it again.

3. In the "Stop attack partner when his HP (%) is smaller than" box, enter the amount of HP in percent your partner should have when you stop attacking for them to heal.

4. Click on "Active" to turn on the auto Trainer.

 

Partner Info - This tool is used to show you how much Health your partner has currently.
