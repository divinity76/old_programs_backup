Info - Shows you your character's current informations

 

Bot Status - Turns the bot fully on or fully off.

1. Clicking "Actived" will turn the bot fully on.

2. Clicking "Disabled" will turn the bot fully off.

 

Light - Makes your screen brighter or darker without any need for a torch.

1. Drag the bar to make the light more or less intense.

2. Clicking "Active" will enable and disable this function.

 

Chameleon - Will make you (or anyone on screen) look like any type of creature (only you will see this, not anyone else).

1. In the "Character" box, type the name of the person/creature that you want to change.

2. Click on the arrow pointing down to see a list of creatures you can change to. Select the one you want to be.

3. Click on "Change" to make the person's appearance change.

4. Click on "Me" and the bot will automatically type your name into the "Character" box.

 

Other Things - These are the other things in the "Main" tab that weren't under any specific category.

1. Show Experience To Next Level As Soul Points - Click on this and where your Soul Points are normally displayed in the Tibia window, you will see the experience you need for the next level.

2. Hide To Tray - By clicking this button, the bot will minimize itself into the little bar thats usually at the bottom-right corner of your screen.

3. Exit (MPA Bot) - By clicking this button, you will close the bot completely.
