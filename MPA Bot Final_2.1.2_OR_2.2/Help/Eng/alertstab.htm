Left Column - These tools will let you set how your character will respond to other players when you are away from your computer.

1. Under "When Player", you can select what will trigger your character to respond.

2. Click on the circle that is the direction that you want your character to move when Step 1 is activated. (You will only move 1 square/space).

3. By selecting the "Move Back" box, your character will step back to his starting position when the threat is gone.

4. By selecting the "Detect all Floors" box, your character will move when a person walks by underneath you and above you as well as in your screen area.

5. By selecting the "Logout when someone enters screen" box, your character will Logout upon another person walking on screen.

6. By selecting the "Use Safe List" box, your character will not respond when a person in your Safe List walks into your area.

7. Click on "Active" to turn on the Left Column.

 

Middle Column - This tool will let you set characters that can be near you without setting off any function in the "Alerts" tab.

1. In the "Safe List" box, you will see names of people that you have allowed to be near you without setting off your characters run-away choices.

2. By clicking the "Clear List" button, you will delete ALL names that you currently have in your Safe List.

3. By clicking the "Load from VIP" button, you will cause the Safe List to be all of the names that you have in your characters VIP list.

4. When you click the "Delete" button, you will erase the name that you have currently selected in your Safe List.

5. By clicking the "Insert" button, you will be prompted by a pop-up window to enter/add a name of your choice to the Safe List.

6. When you click the "Load from Screen" button, the Bot will put the name of every character on your screen into the Safe List.

 

Right Column - This tool will let you set a few special things for your character to do.

1. By selecting the "Logout when no blanks" box, your character will log himself out as soon as you run out of blank runes.

2. By selecting the "Active Above Functions" box, you will turn on the "Logout when no blanks" function.

3. By selecting the "Play sound when player Enter your screen" box, your computer will play a sound upon a character showing up near you, so you know that they are there.

4. By clicking the "Play Sound" button, your computer will play an example of the alert sound to you so you know how loud you need to set your volume.
